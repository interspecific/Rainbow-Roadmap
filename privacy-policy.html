Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2025

App Name: Rainbow Roadmap

Developer: Rainbow Roadmap

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Rainbow Roadmap (“the App”) handles user privacy. The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is important to us, and we ensure that the App operates with transparency and security.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Rainbow Roadmap does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal data (such as name, email, phone number).
  • Device information, IP addresses, or cookies.
  • Usage analytics or tracking data.
  • Any other personally identifiable information (PII).

3. Location Access (Optional)

The App provides an optional feature that allows users to enable location services:

  • This feature only accesses your device's location when you manually enable it.
  • The location data is used only within the app to zoom to your current position.
  • No location data is stored, transmitted, or shared with any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Rainbow Roadmap does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Security

Since the App does not collect any personal data, there is no risk of data breaches or misuse of your information.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
