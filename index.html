Rainbow Roadmap

An Interactive Map and Resource Hub of LGBTQ, Climate, Environmental, Health, and Economic Data

  • View and Explore Layers: Click the "Map Layers" button in the top-right corner of the map. Use the drop-down arrows to expand groups, and click the eye icon to turn layers on or off.
  • Zoom and Visibility: If a layer appears grayed out, zoom in or out until it becomes visible. Some layers only appear at certain zoom levels.
  • Click on the Map: Select features on the map to open a pop-up with detailed data from that layer.
  • Learn About the Data: Open the "Data Sources" section in the menu for links to the datasets used in this project.
  • Need Help or See an Issue? Find our contact info in the menu.
