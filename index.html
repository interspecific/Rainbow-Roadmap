Rainbow Roadmap

An Interactive Map and Resource Hub of LGBTQ, Climate, Environmental, Health, and Economic Data

This tool empowers you to make informed decisions about travel and relocation in the face of political hostility and climate change in the United States.

How to Use This Tool

  • Explore the Map: Toggle layers on and off, zoom to different scales, and click on geographic features to view the underlying attribute information.
  • Click "Layer List and Legend" button in the top-right corner. Expand layer groups to see individual data layers, and use the "eye" icon to toggle visibility. If a layer is greyed out, zoom in or out to make it visible.
  • Interact with Features: Click on a feature to see detailed information in a pop-up table.
  • Explore Data Sources: Visit the Data Sources section in the menu to access data sources used in this tool.
  • Report Issues: Use the contact information in the header to report errors or suggest improvements.
