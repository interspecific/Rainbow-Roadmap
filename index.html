Rainbow Roadmap

An Interactive Map of LGBTQ, Climate, Environmental, Health, and Economic Data

This tool can be used to help make informed decisions when traveling and relocating in times of political hostility and climate change in the US.

Try:

  • Toggling layers on and off, zooming to different scales, and clicking on geographic features to see the underlying attribute information.
  • Click the layers list and legend button on the top right. Toggle layers on and off using the icon on the left. If a layer does not show up or is greyed out, try zooming in our out. Toggle legend details on the right next to layer names.
  • Zoom to a location on the map to see how the data layers are visualized, and click on a feature to see the additional data associated with that feature.
  • Submitting a new safe location to the database using the button on the bottom left.
